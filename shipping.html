Common Questions

What areas do you deliver to?

We currently deliver to most urban areas within our operating region. Enter your address at checkout to check availability.

Can I schedule a delivery time?

Yes! During checkout, you can choose your preferred delivery time slot based on availability.

What if I'm not home during delivery?

Our drivers will contact you. If unavailable, we'll leave your order in a safe, shaded place and send photo confirmation.

Do you deliver in bad weather?

We deliver in most weather conditions. In case of extreme weather, we'll contact you to reschedule.

Need Help?

Our support team is here to assist with any delivery questions.
