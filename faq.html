Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our products and services

Products

Yes! Every product in our selection is 100% organic certified. We work exclusively with farms that practice sustainable, chemical-free agriculture and maintain rigorous organic certification standards.

We deliver within 24 hours of harvest. Our products are picked at peak ripeness and transported in temperature-controlled vehicles to maintain optimal freshness from farm to your table.

We partner with 50+ local farms within our region. This allows us to support local communities while ensuring the shortest possible time between harvest and delivery.

Delivery

We deliver daily from 8 AM to 8 PM across most urban areas. Next-day delivery is available for orders placed before 6 PM. You can check specific delivery availability during checkout.

Yes! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed before 12 PM. Availability may vary based on your location and current delivery capacity.

Our drivers will attempt to contact you. If unavailable, we'll leave your order in a safe, shaded place and send you a photo confirmation. For perishable items, we recommend scheduling delivery when you're home.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day freshness guarantee. If you're not satisfied with the quality or freshness of any product, contact us within 30 days for a full refund or replacement.

Contact our support team within 30 days of delivery. We'll arrange pickup of the unsatisfactory items and process your refund or replacement within 2-3 business days.

Due to health and safety regulations, we cannot accept returns of opened or partially consumed food items. However, we'll gladly address any quality concerns you may have.

Account & Orders

You can modify your order within 1 hour of placement by contacting our support team. After 1 hour, orders enter our fulfillment process and cannot be changed.
